# Document 1A: Singapore Private Residential Research - Meyer Road (District 15)

**Date:** March 2026
**Target Audience:** Real Estate Executive Summary

---

## **BOTTOM LINE UP FRONT (BLUF): EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**
Meyer Road remains the primary **"Blue Chip"** investment corridor of Singapore’s East Coast.

*   **Investability High:** Proximity to the Central Business District (CBD) and unblocked sea views provide high asset resilience.
*   **Sales Velocity:** Units in this corridor exhibit high absorption rates. **Meyer Blue** (2024 launch) sold **50% of units on launch day**, while boutique projects like **One Meyer** and **Meyerhouse** are **100% sold out**, indicating intense demand for new freehold supply.
*   **Infrastructure Catalyst:** The completion of the **Thomson-East Coast Line (TEL)** has bridged the pricing gap between the East Coast and the Core Central Region (CCR).
*   **Asset Scarcity:** Only 12 freehold plots with direct seafront access exist on Meyer Road; all are now developed, ensuring no future direct competition.

### Comp Analysis & Rationale

*   **Pricing Outliers:** **Meyerhouse** is a significant pricing outlier in terms of absolute quantum (S$ 5M+ entry) due to its "no small units" strategy. While PSF is comparable to other luxury projects, the entry barrier is significantly higher.
*   **Pricing Outlier:** **Coastline Residences** has seen transactions exceeding S$ 3,300 PSF for high-floor small units, reflecting a high premium for unblocked sea views despite smaller site area.
*   **Yield Comparison:** **One Meyer** and **LIV @ MB** are expected to outperform in rental yield percentage due to their unit mix (abundance of 1 and 2-bedroom units) compared to the larger-format "family" units in Meyer Road projects.
