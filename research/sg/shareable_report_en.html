3. Investment Analysis

Capital Appreciation

High potential driven by tenure (Freehold) and infrastructure completion. Meyer Road is one of the few residential enclaves in Singapore with direct park and sea access while being <10 mins from the CBD.

Rental Yield

Estimated 2.8% - 3.5%. Yield is optimized in boutique projects like One Meyer due to unit mix efficiency. Larger format luxury units in Meyerhouse prioritize capital preservation over immediate yield.

4. Legal Process for Taiwanese Nationals

Taiwanese investors are permitted to buy non-landed residential property (Condos) in Singapore. Note: A 60% Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty (ABSD) applies to foreign nationals as of current cooling measures.
